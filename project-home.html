About Us

Lode Stone Valley Country Club was established in 1935 by George Stone, an active and passionate golfer. Today, thanks to his guidance and direction, the course now features some of the finest green grass a golfer could ask for. Our green is the greenest you could ever lay eyes on. The fairways are both difficult and rewarding when you make a successful drive. We're excited to see if you can conquer our well-maintained and challenging course.
