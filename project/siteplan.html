Project Plan

By: Christopher Scott

Site Name:

Lode Stone Valley Country Club - The reason I chose this name is because first off it is a fake town I came up with, and the second reason is because I did a quick search on if there was a Load Stone Valley Country Club, and this domain seems pretty open to use. It also is a very good name for the site because it shows simply what the site is about, which is a golf course site.

Site Purpose:

This site provides an overview of what the Lode Stone Valley Country Club is about. What the course is like, what the holes are like, and what the golfers that come are like. It also allows a user to sign up for a membership of their choice to the club. It also allows people to view pricing information like how much a 9-hole course would be verses a 18-hole course. What the prices of golf carts is like, and other things like that on the site.

Scenarios:

  • How can Lode Stone Country Club give me a good weekend experiance that I can remember?
  • How can I become a member of the club?
  • What makes Lode Stone Country Club different from other golf courses?

Color Schema:

Moss Green (#8A9A5B) - Header, Footer, and other highlight on site.

Dark Green (#013220) - Background color, and used pretty minimal on site.

Chocolate Brown (#a36a29) - Nav Bar.

White (#fff) - Main, Nav font color, and most places on site where reading is required.

Black (#000) - Used mainly for fonts for reading.

Typography:

Rethink Sans - Used on Header, Nav, and Header elements.

Intel One Mono - Used on pretty much everything else in the site like the p tags and other words.
